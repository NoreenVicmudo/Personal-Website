About

Yaloo! It's nice to meet a fellow, I'm Slychan ♪ I am an artist who mostly draw fanarts. This website will focus on sharing my fanart and own works. As well as providing services for others who wants to have an exclusive artwork. The actual purpose of using this website is to support my bills and to upgrade my equipments little by little. As long as I can save for future purposes while also developing my skills in drawing and offer exclusive stuffs to those who supports me. Unfortunately, I'm not really that confident since it's just been a while since I started doing digital art.

However, I got interested when others started requesting me to draw this and that which can be a little tricky and takes time. So I thought about if I set up a paywall so if they want something special, they'll have to pay for it while I'll also feel rewarded too? I guess haha, since the requests sometimes takes quite an effort. I do know that in future, you don't do stuffs just for experience.

So that's all, Thank you for checking out this page! If you ever uh decide to support me, you can view some of the exclusive and pay extra if you want your very own Slychan-made artwork created? hehe ( * 3 * )
